# disco-reggae-live-energy-skill

## Purpose
Use this skill to make disco-reggae outputs feel embodied, social, and alive.

This module is about:
- human lift
- communal feel
- band-like interplay where appropriate
- call and response
- audience warmth
- movement readability

## When to use
Use this skill when:
- the result feels too programmed
- the user wants more live warmth
- the hybrid needs more human celebration
- a performance-oriented version is needed

## Workflow
1. Choose live-energy source.
   - percussion looseness
   - bass phrasing
   - vocal call and response
   - guitar/keys interplay
   - crowd-facing chorus lift
   - horn punctuation

2. Choose body signal.
   - hip sway
   - rolling bounce
   - open-hand celebration
   - slow warm grind
   - communal singalong

3. Choose imperfection level.
   - polished-live
   - lightly human
   - jam-friendly
   - dub-console reactive

## If/Then rules
- If the result feels cold, add human timing nuance or vocal interaction.
- If the result feels messy, reduce simultaneous humanization layers.
- If dance motion is weak, strengthen the body signal.
- If the track becomes too jam-band, restore discipline and groove shape.

## Guardrails
- Avoid fake “island party” clichés.
- Avoid over-loose timing.
- Avoid forcing live-band chaos into tight dance cuts.
- Avoid exaggerated crowd tropes unless requested.

## Output format
Return:
- Live-energy source
- Body signal
- Imperfection level
- Communal factor
- What to avoid
